Create and sign agreements using natural language

Our AI-powered platform uses natural language chat models to simplify the agreement creation and signing process, guiding your signees through the agreement terms, answering any questions, and ensuring deliverables are fulfilled. Smarter contracts to automate your agreements

A custom, verifiable smart contract will be generated to automate your signing workflow. It can be instructed to trigger various actions when conditions are met, such as notififying external systems, processing payments, issuing shipping instructions, and more.

Permanent, tamper-proof transaction audit trail

A secure, transparent, and tamper-proof ledger of all agreement transactions will be recorded on the blockchain, ensuring a verifiable and immutable record of evidence. The transaction audit trail will be linked to the signees’ transcripts, forming a legally binding agreement.

How is Contrabt better than current web2 signing systems?

Contrabt outperforms traditional Web2 signing platforms with superior AI usability, advanced automation through smart contracts, and significantly stronger security powered by blockchain technology.

What is the difference between the basic and paid versions?

The basic version is perfect for straightforward agreement creation and signing. For more advanced workflows — such as system integrations, task automation, file analysis for answering questions, multi-party push notifications, and more — the paid version is the ideal choice.

What experience does the team have?

Our team has spent the last 12 years as senior engineers and tech leads in the Web2 electronic signatures industry, recently advancing these systems with blockchain technology. We believe that combining AI with distributed ledger technology is the next evolution in overcoming the industry’s limitations.

Are the contracts legally binding?

Yes. Our platform meets the regulatory requirements of EU's eIDAS Advanced Electronic Signatures (AES) and the US equivalent ESIGN act, making them legally binding.

How is my data secured?

Once signed, your transcripts are encrypted and securely stored on IPFS (or a custom file system), accessible only to you and your signees. An immutable, tamper-proof transaction audit trail is recorded on-chain, ensuring verifiable proof of the agreement’s current state.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards and crypto payments. In the future we may also offer token gated access to our product. You have the option to pay monthly or receive discounts if you pay yearly. We also offer corporate access to unlimited and API product usage, please get in touch for more information.

Do you have a token?

No, we currently do not have a token, but this may change in the future. Please follow our socials to keep up-to-date with our latest news releases.
